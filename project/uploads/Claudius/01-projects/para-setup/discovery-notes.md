**Weekday evenings:** Come home (hopefully after gym if it was an office day). Video games, cook/prep dinner, watch TV with Tim while eating, journal before bed. On RBG days, rhythm shifts around that.

**Sunday mornings:** Ideally the Buddy Meeting with Tim (finances, meal planning, important dates, mutual support). Currently lapsed. Tim sometimes has softball or basketball which disrupts it when it does get scheduled.

## What's Broken

**The core problem:** No early warning system for energy/capacity. Katie doesn't notice she's at the far edge of red until she's already there. Personal and mental hygiene fall first when things are slipping.

**Energy framework:** Green > Yellow > Orange > Red. Goal is to stop consistently at orange before hitting red. She's rarely catching orange.

**Other gaps:**
- Buddy Meetings with Tim: fallen out of habit (inertia + Tim's variable weekend schedule)
- Music: nothing logged since August 2025
- Zettelkasten: consistency varies
- Mornings: no time or structure before work

---

## Signal System (What to Watch For)

**Smooth Brain Mode frequency:** If Katie activates it more than ~3x/week, that's probably orange or heading toward red. Track this across conversations.

**Value drift:** When Katie does things that contradict her stated values, that's a meaningful signal. Call it out — it's a canary for declining mental health or energy. This is more reliable than absence of mentions of Tim, Maggie, or Buddy Meetings (those aren't necessarily indicators).

## Core Philosophy (Her Words, Saved Exactly)

True play is the ability to be curious enough to make something you've seen a thousand times enrapture your curiosity. This is a core value she wants held through all assistance.

**Practical implication:** The job is NOT to find the next perfect tool or system. It's to help her work within the framework she's already built — Notion, Obsidian, Google Calendar, Caveday, paper journal — and bring curiosity and consistency to what's already there.

She spent years chasing silver bullets. That era is over.

## AI Relationship

**Tools used:** ChatGPT for work (still active). Claude personally, paid subscriber. No bad experiences worth cataloguing — the main trust issue is hallucinated sources.

**Source rule:** Always provide sources when possible. Always verify they actually exist before citing them. This is non-negotiable for trust.

**Visual preference:** Strong visual thinker. Appreciates interactive UI, dialog boxes, visual organization. Does NOT want image generation. Interactivity and visual structure are always welcome.

**Technical level:** Codes but not heavily. Knows HTML, SQL, some Python. Open to learning more over time through our work together.

**The dream:** A human executive assistant. Specifically — offloading the mental load of running a home. Appointments, things not slipping through cracks, the invisible labor that drains before the real work even starts.

**Hard limits:**
- No financial stuff
- No acting on her behalf without asking first — always suggest, never execute unilaterally

**Friction threshold / exit condition:** The goal is actually to need Claude less over time. She wants the scaffolding built well enough that she internalizes the systems and reduces dependency. Build toward her independence, not her reliance.
